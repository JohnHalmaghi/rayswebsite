    • About Me

    My name is Ray Blair. I am 34 years old married to my beautiful wife Jen, And we have 3 great but crazy kids ages 6, 4 , 2. I was born in San Diego, CA and lived there most of my life. I was not raised in a christian home. Parents divorced when I was 9 and eventually became a high school drop out, choosing the party and drug life. But when I was 19 God opened my eyes through a series of events to my need of Him and the truth and power of the bible. I surrendered my life at that point and hit the ground running. I was seemingly instantly a new creation in Christ and born again. God began changing me from the inside out and became almost unrecognizable to those who knew me before. Within my first year as a christian i enrolled in a missions/evangelism school and was immersed in the bible and christian community. I was immediately evangelizing and going on countless missions trips. by age 22 I had sold what little possessions I had to walk by faith as a missionary in Mexico. I began a young adult ministry and had 30+ attending the bible study in Puerto Vallarta Mexico. I soon realized my need to know more of Gods word and felt it was time to move back for personal growth. I attended a bible college but was discontent with how difficult they made the bible out to be. So I dropped out and began studying chapter and verse book by book doing exegesis. I made use of many bible tools to aid my study of the original languages, and to help me in my studying of Gods word. While still doing short term missions trips I began to lead a home bible study for young adults. It was then I found my passion for personal discipleship. It has now been 12 years since I have been doing personal discipleship and have seen these men become Pastors, church planters, and leaders in their churches doing the same type of ministry. God has used this introverted boy and turned Him into a man who has a passion to speak Gods word to as many people as possible. I have had the joy of preaching and serving in various pastoral roles during this time, but my greatest joy is to disciple and minister to individuals. To help people Know God and to make Him known. My heart is to love God with all I am and to live in enjoyment of constant fellowship and communion with Him. I have since obtained a bachelors in theology and have been ordained by a Southern Baptist Church.
